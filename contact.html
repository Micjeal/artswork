Getting Here

By Subway:

Take the N, Q, R, W, S, 1, 2, 3, or 7 train to Times Square-42nd Street. Walk 5 minutes south to our location.

By Bus:

Multiple bus lines serve the area including M7, M20, M42, and M104.

By Car:

Limited street parking available. We recommend using nearby parking garages.

Accessibility:

Our gallery is fully wheelchair accessible with elevator access to all floors.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book tickets in advance?

General admission is free and no booking is required. However, special exhibitions and workshops may require advance booking.

Can I take photos in the gallery?

Photography is allowed in most areas for personal use. Flash photography and tripods are not permitted. Some special exhibitions may have photography restrictions.

Do you offer group tours?

Yes, we offer guided tours for groups of 10 or more. Please contact us at least 2 weeks in advance to arrange a tour.

Is there a gift shop?

Yes, our gift shop features books, prints, and unique items related to our exhibitions and artists. It's open during regular gallery hours.

Can I rent the space for events?

We offer venue rental for corporate events, private parties, and special occasions. Contact us for availability and pricing.

Do you accept artwork submissions?

We review submissions from emerging and established artists. Please send your portfolio and artist statement to exhibitions@artgallery.com.
